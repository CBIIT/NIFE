Welcome to the NCI Imaging Facilities Environment!

NIFE is an NCI-wide microscopy image management resource jointly developed and maintained by the NCI Imaging Facilities it supports and CBIIT. From this page, you can submit metadata for samples to be imaged and then access the resulting images for further analysis.

Use the Links on the left side bar to learn more about working with each of the Imaging Facilities and upload metadata
